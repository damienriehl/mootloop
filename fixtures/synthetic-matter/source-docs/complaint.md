# Complaint

STATE OF MINNESOTA
COUNTY OF RICE — THIRD JUDICIAL DISTRICT

Northfield Widgets LLC, Plaintiff, v. Granite Supply Co., Defendant.
Case No. 66-CV-26-0417

Plaintiff Northfield Widgets LLC, for its Complaint against Defendant Granite Supply
Co., alleges as follows:

## Parties and Jurisdiction

1. Plaintiff is a Minnesota limited liability company with its principal place of
   business in Northfield, Rice County, Minnesota.

2. Defendant is a Minnesota corporation engaged in the supply of dimensional stone
   components.

## Facts

3. On January 3, 2026, the parties entered into a written Supply Agreement for 500
   granite widget assemblies.

4. Defendant tendered the assemblies on or about March 14, 2026.

5. Upon inspection, at least 120 of the assemblies exhibited hairline cracks
   rendering them unusable.

6. Plaintiff timely notified Defendant of the defects and rejected the shipment.

## Count I — Breach of Contract

7. Defendant breached the Supply Agreement by tendering nonconforming Goods.

8. As a direct result, Plaintiff has been damaged in an amount exceeding $50,000.

WHEREFORE, Plaintiff demands judgment against Defendant for damages, interest, costs,
and such other relief as the Court deems just.
