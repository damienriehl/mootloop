# Granite Widget Supply Agreement

This Supply Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of January 3, 2026, by and
between Granite Supply Co. ("Seller") and Northfield Widgets LLC ("Buyer").

## 1. Goods

The Goods consist of 500 granite widget assemblies conforming to Specification GS-11.
Each assembly shall be free from cracks, chips, and structural defects at the time of
tender.

## 2. Price

Buyer shall pay Seller the sum of $148,500 for the Goods. Payment is due net thirty
(30) days after delivery and acceptance.

## 3. Delivery

Seller shall deliver all Goods to Buyer's Northfield facility on or before March 15,
2026. Delivery is complete upon tender at Buyer's loading dock.

## 4. Inspection and Acceptance

Buyer shall have ten (10) business days after tender to inspect the Goods and to
notify Seller in writing of any nonconformity. Failure to notify within that period
constitutes acceptance.

## 5. Governing Law

This Agreement is governed by the laws of the State of Minnesota, including the
Uniform Commercial Code as adopted in Minnesota.
