# Answer and Affirmative Defenses

STATE OF MINNESOTA
COUNTY OF RICE — THIRD JUDICIAL DISTRICT

Northfield Widgets LLC, Plaintiff, v. Granite Supply Co., Defendant.
Case No. 66-CV-26-0417

Defendant Granite Supply Co., for its Answer to the Complaint, states as follows:

## Responses

1. Defendant admits the parties entered into a written Supply Agreement dated
   January 3, 2026.

2. Granite Supply tendered all 500 assemblies on March 14, 2026, one day before the
   deadline.

3. Defendant denies that any assemblies were nonconforming at the time of tender.

4. Defendant affirmatively alleges that any cracks arose from Buyer's mishandling
   during offloading.

## Affirmative Defenses

5. Plaintiff's claims are barred, in whole or in part, because Plaintiff failed to
   provide timely written notice of nonconformity as required by the Agreement.
   Acceptance and additional terms are governed by Minn. Stat. § 336.2-207, and
   discovery is governed by Minn. R. Civ. P. 33.01. See Nordwind v. Cassini, 512
   N.W.2d 999 (Minn. 1994).

6. Plaintiff's damages, if any, were caused by Plaintiff's own conduct.

WHEREFORE, Defendant requests that the Complaint be dismissed with prejudice and that
Defendant be awarded its costs and disbursements.
